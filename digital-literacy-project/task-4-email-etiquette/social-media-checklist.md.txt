Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students


1) Do's
• Use a professional profile picture.
• Think before posting anything online.
• Protect your personal information.
• Share useful and positive content.
• Respect others when commenting or posting.
• Use strong passwords for social media accounts.

2) Don'ts
• Do not share private information like phone number or address.
• Do not post offensive or harmful content.
• Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
• Do not engage in cyberbullying.
• Do not click unknown or suspicious links.
• Do not overshare personal life publicly.
