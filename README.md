# Digital Literacy Project

## Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project created as part of the **CSE0001 – Digital Literacy course**.
The purpose of this project is to understand important concepts related to digital skills, online safety, responsible internet usage, and professional communication in the digital world.

The project includes multiple tasks such as creating a presentation, designing an infographic portfolio, analyzing digital platforms, learning proper email etiquette, and understanding cybercrime awareness.

Through these activities, the project helps in developing practical digital literacy skills that are essential for students in today's technology-driven environment.

## Tasks Included

### Task 1 – Presentation

A presentation explaining key concepts of digital literacy and responsible digital behavior.

### Task 2 – Portfolio

An infographic created to represent digital safety awareness and important online practices.

### Task 3 – Platforms

Analysis of different digital platforms and how they are used for communication, learning, and information sharing.

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Explanation of proper email etiquette along with an example of a professional email format.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Information about different types of cybercrime and steps that can be taken to stay safe online.
